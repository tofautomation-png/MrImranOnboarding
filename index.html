Below is your onboarding guide — from creating your custom avatar to scripting, batch video production, and automated scheduling for a full month of posts. Please share the requested details via info@targetime.com or WhatsApp.

Project Window: 7 working days

01 · Custom Avatar — Recording Requirements

Please record 2–5 minutes of high-resolution video (1080p or 4K) in a well-lit, quiet space. Look directly at the camera, speak naturally, pause with your mouth closed between sentences, and keep hand gestures below chest level.

Live Consent: We’ll also capture a short consent video. Please be available for a 10-minute call during avatar creation.

02 · Audience Profile (7 Key Inputs)

These will steer scripts, copy, visuals, and tone. Bullet replies are perfect.

  1. Primary Audience Segment — first-time buyers, investors, luxury sellers, renters.
  2. Location & Market Tier — target communities and price range (mid/upper-mid/luxury).
  3. Buyer Pain Points — top 3 problems (financing, paperwork, market confusion, time).
  4. Desired Outcomes — fast closings, best ROI, low hassle, exclusive deals.
  5. Tone & Persona — energetic / expert / concierge / mentor; language mix (EN/AR/UR).
  6. Content Themes — market tips, area spotlights, investment math, listings, testimonials.
  7. Visual Direction — minimal vs dynamic, on-screen text density, b-roll preferences.

Send to: info@targetime.com or WhatsApp.

03 · Third-Party Access (Facebook/Instagram/TikTok)

We’ll need publishing access strictly for automated posting. Choose one:

  • Option A: Receive official step-by-step documents and grant access at your convenience.
  • Option B: A quick 15-minute screen-share to set it up live and confirm permissions.

04 · Timeline (7 Working Days)

Sequenced milestones from avatar to full-month automation.

Day 1

Kickoff & Inputs

  • Confirm 7 audience inputs & language/tone

  • Choose access method (docs or live)
Day 2

Avatar Capture

  • Record 2–5 min (1080p/4K)

Day 3

Scripting

Day 5

Batch Video Production

Day 6

Access & Automation Setup

  • Confirm Meta/IG/TikTok permissions
  • Connect schedulers & set posting cadence
  • QA posting previews (no live yet)
Day 7

Scheduling & Final QA

  • Schedule the full month of posts
  • Final quality assurance pass
  • Handover summary (posting plan, asset index)

Notes: Working days exclude weekends/public holidays. If approvals or access are delayed, the schedule adapts without changing the sequence.
